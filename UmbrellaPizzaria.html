About Us

All of Rihanna's life she knew that she wanted to own a pizza shop. She started off working in small pizzerias around her neighborhood until one day she had saved up enough to open up her own pizza shop. At first it didn't do too well considering that no one had heard of Rihanna before and was skeptic to try a pizza from a new pizzeria when the neighborhood had been so used to going to one pizzeria. However, Rihanna didn't give up. She continued to pursue people and advertise her business. One day a bunch of kids came in and tried her pizza and that was the start of her business. Rihanna's Pizza became the new hang out spot for kids to come and eat pizza after a long day of school.
